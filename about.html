How we got here

One kitchen. One Ghost Pepper. One regrettable but life-changing evening. From that humble (and hot) start, we started asking questions: where do these peppers come from, who grows the best seeds, and how can we share them with curious cooks and home gardeners? The answer: SpiceUp! — a global tiny-seed-to-giant-flavor operation.
